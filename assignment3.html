Porsche GT3

The all-new Porsche 911 GT3 has been completely redesigned, it is now powered by a 4.0 liter naturally aspirated in-line 6 cylinder. It puts out 502 horsepower and 346 pound-feet of torque. The engine has a redline at a whopping 9000 rpm, which sounds absolutely incredible. Thanks to the massive wing out back it is able to generate up to 150% more downforce than the previous model.
